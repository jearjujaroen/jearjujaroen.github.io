About Me

My name is Jearrranai Jujaroen, but you can call me Jear (rhymes with Bear). My family and I moved from Bangkok, Thailand to Portland, ME at the age of 8 years old to be with my grandmother. I grew up in a family-owned restaurant business and worked there for most of my life, then worked in sales and social-media marketing until I found my true calling in programming. I set off on my own path and I have not looked back since.

What I Do

I am a full stack engineer who is passionate about making an impact in the world. I have a strong background in building JavaScript web applications using a variety of modern frontend frameworks such as React.js/Redux, AngularJS and Node.js/Express.js, SQL, and NoSQL databases for the backend. I value writing clean and modular code while incorporating build automation and debugging tools for a smooth development flow.

Looking forward to solving problems and building great things with you and your team!
